Beginner Tips

More Fitness Insights

How often should I work out?

For beginners, aim for 3–4 sessions a week focusing on full-body movements. Rest days are essential for recovery and preventing injuries.

How can I stay motivated?

Track small wins, celebrate progress, and find a workout buddy. Remember, motivation grows with action — not before it!

What's a good pre-workout snack?

Opt for something light with carbs and protein — like a banana with peanut butter or a smoothie with oats and milk.
